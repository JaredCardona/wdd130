My Favorite City is Quito

Ecuador, Quito

Quito, Ecuador, is a fascinating destination, boasting one of the best-preserved historic centers in the Americas, being the capital city closest to the equator, and offering breathtaking Andean landscapes with volcanoes reaching 4,000 meters above sea level. Its blend of colonial culture, local cuisine, art, and nature makes it a leading destination in South America.
