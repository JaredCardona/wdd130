The Window to the World is a public monument located in Barranquilla, Colombia. It was built in late 2018 to coincide with the XXIII Central American and Caribbean Games, which the city hosted. It is situated in a roundabout on the Circunvalar industrial corridor, near its intersection with Vía 40. The work is the work of Barranquilla architect Diana Escorcia Borelly. Construction was initiated by the company Tecnoglass in collaboration with other companies such as Alutions by Tecnoglass, Aconstruir, Kuraray-Trosifol, Argos, M&P Distribuciones Valledupar (JLQG), GCC, Dow, Alloy, Alutrafic Led, Escenarios Deportivos, Smart Steel, as well as the Barranquilla District Mayor's Office.
